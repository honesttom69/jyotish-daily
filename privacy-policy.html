Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Summary

Your birth data never leaves your device. Jyotish Daily stores all personal information locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server.

What Data We Store

Jyotish Daily stores the following information in your browser's local storage:

  • Name (as you enter it)
  • Birth date, time, and place
  • Calculated chart data
  • Premium license status

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

External Services

The app makes two types of external requests:

  • Geocoding (OpenStreetMap Nominatim): When you enter a birth place, we send the location name to OpenStreetMap to retrieve coordinates. No personal information is included in this request.
  • License Verification (Gumroad): If you purchase premium, we send your license key to Gumroad's API to verify the purchase. No birth data is included.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies. We do not collect usage data.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, clear your browser's local storage for this site, or uninstall the app. Since we don't store anything on servers, there's nothing else to delete.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
